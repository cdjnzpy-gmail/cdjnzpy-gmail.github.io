July 6–10, 2026 • Beijing, China
Format: In-person | Language: English

This summer school offers an intensive series of methodological lectures and tutorials designed to equip early-career researchers (including PhD students and junior faculty) and exceptional students with theoretical foundations and practical applications in deep learning and macro-finance.

Focus Areas:

  • ✦ Foundations of deep learning & computational tools
  • ✦ Heterogeneous-agent macroeconomic models
  • ✦ Maliar–Maliar–Winant methods
  • ✦ Deep Equilibrium Networks
  • ✦ DeepHAM & structural reinforcement learning
  • ✦ Continuous-time macro-finance & PDE methods

* Includes hands-on tutorials emphasizing concepts to code.

Post-Summer School Conference

Following the summer school, attend a two-day conference (July 11–12) bringing together leading academics to discuss recent advancements in machine learning and macroeconomics.
